about me

I am a creative and empathetic designer who is passionate about using visual design to improve people’s daily lives.

I’m always excited about how my design work can align with my interests in mental health and environmental sustainability.

My background in painting and photography informs my design work in an innovative and visually exciting way.

skills

UX research

I do my best to do objective and thorough research to focus on the needs of the user for each project.

User empathy

An empathetic person at heart, I use my listening and communication skills to help me understand how my designs can help my users most efficiently.

Qualitative interviewing

During user interviews, I combine an empathetic and exploratory approach with gathering data through surveys to best understand a project's design goals.
